# Game Session Template (Chat)

Copy this into chat to initialize a new game.

---

## 1️⃣ Coin Toss

- Player A: Heads / Tails
- Player B: Heads / Tails
- Coin result:
- Winner chooses: Draw 8 cards from the pile and Put down 1 card / Allow other player to draw 8 cards from the pile and put down 1 card

---

## 2️⃣ Player 1 / Player 2

- Play a card based on what day it is and what garbage is being picked up
- or Play any card on a day when no garbage will be picked up

---

## 3️⃣ Win Condition 

- ☐ First player to lose all of their cards

---

## 4️⃣ Time Rules 

- 1 round is 1 day, each player gets to play a card or challenge during their turn
- 7 days in a week, with 3-4 days where garbage is picked up
- Each player gets 2 wild cards, which are **Oversleep** and **Holiday**
- Oversleep means 1 other player "oversleeps" can't put out the garbage for their turn or challenge. It is played at the end of the turn.
- The Holiday card means all garbage pickup is delayed by 1 day, so pickup days for the next week (or 7 days) are shifted by 1 day.

---

## 5️⃣ Board Position

- The game starts on a Monday
- One round is one day, with each player completing their turns to advance to the next day
- Garbage is picked up on the day scheduled, and then Granny checks the cards for any illegal garbage items.
- If Granny finds any wrong items in the garbage, she places them face up in the correct category.

---

## 6️⃣ Card Secrecy Protocol (Required)

- Cards are placed face down in the category chosen by the player.
- Cards are not revealed unless challenged by another player at the beginning of their turn.
- If a challenge is successful, then all cards in the pile are picked up by the player that lost the challenge.

---

## 7️⃣ Rules when playing AI

- Both the Human player and the AI player take 8 cards each from the deck.
- There are 52 cards in the deck, including 2 Oversleep cards and 2 Holiday cards, with 48 cards representing items disposed of as garbage by households.
- There are 4 categories of garbage to be disposed of: Burnable, Non-burnable, Plastics and Recycling
- The AI player will remember the cards it receives and that the Human player receives. 
- The AI player will not "see" cards that the Human player places facedown on the board.
- The AI player may challenge the Human player's cards played in a category if it decides there is a high probability of winning the challenge.
- The AI player may bluff by putting an item card in the wrong category facedown if it will help win the game.
- The bluff will be revealed after the garbage is picked up on the correct day and the granny is checking the garbage. At that time the incorrectly placed item is placed back on the board face up in the correct category.

---

## ✅ Session Locked

- Players put a card face down in a category at the beginning of their turn.
- Players can choose to put a card down or challenge a previous card put down by a player.
- After the garbage is picked up on a day, and the cards are moved off the board, a granny (played by the AI) checks the cards to see if they are accurate for the disposal category.
- If not, it is announced and they are returned to the board in the correct category.
